# Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn data to identify the key factors contributing to customer attrition and quantify the business impact of churn. Using SQL for data exploration and Power BI for visualization, the dashboard provides actionable insights that can help organizations improve customer retention and reduce revenue loss.

---

## Business Problem

Customer churn directly impacts revenue and long-term business growth. The objective of this project is to answer key business questions such as:

* What factors are driving customer churn?
* Which customer groups are most likely to churn?
* How does contract type affect churn?
* Does technical support influence customer retention?
* How much revenue is at risk due to churn?
* How does customer tenure relate to churn behavior?

---

## Project Objectives

* Identify the primary drivers of customer churn
* Compare churn rates by contract type
* Analyze churn rates based on technical support usage
* Calculate revenue lost from churned customers
* Examine churn across different customer tenure groups
* Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for business stakeholders

## Key Insights

### Contract Type

Customers on month-to-month contracts experience significantly higher churn rates compared to customers with one-year or two-year contracts.

### Technical Support

Customers without technical support are much more likely to churn, suggesting that customer support plays a major role in retention.

### Revenue at Risk

Revenue generated by churned customers represents a measurable business risk and highlights the financial impact of customer attrition.

### Customer Tenure

Newer customers experience the highest churn rates, while long-term customers are significantly more likely to remain with the company.
